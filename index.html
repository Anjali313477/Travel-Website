Upcoming Tours & Destination~

Temporary escape from the bustle of everyday life. Forest camping tours, found in destinations like Yosemite National Park in the U.S., the Black Forest in Germany, and the Great Smoky Mountains, provide an opportunity to camp beneath towering trees, hike through lush forests, and experience wildlife in its natural habitat. These tours often include activities such as guided hikes, wildlife spotting, campfire gatherings, and survival skills workshops, offering a well-rounded experience in the heart of nature. The best time to embark on forest camping trips is usually spring and fall, when temperatures are moderate, and wildlife is most active, although winter camping in these forests can be a unique, peaceful experience for those seeking solitude.

Snow trekking, or snowshoeing, on the other hand, invites adventurers into the world of winter landscapes, where snow-covered forests, frozen lakes, and towering mountain peaks set the stage for exploration.
